# Impeccable — Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Two audiences, one backend.
- **Primary (this craft): field technicians** running safety inspections on-site —
  outdoors, often in bright sun, sometimes gloved, time-pressured, phone in one hand.
  The UI must be legible at arm's length in daylight, forgiving of imprecise taps, and
  fast to complete and submit.
- **Secondary: office staff** triaging results from a desk (web `/office`).

### Brand Personality
**Beacon — rugged, trustworthy, fast.** Voice is direct and plain, like good safety
signage and field equipment, not enterprise SaaS. It should feel purpose-built and
dependable: the digital equivalent of a well-worn clipboard and a hi-vis vest.
Restrained — never loud, decorative, or busy.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Restrained industrial / field-instrument.** High contrast, big touch targets, a single
sharp accent. Signage-derived condensed type, tabular numerals, thick rules; structure
over chrome. Warm near-black + stone neutrals (tinted toward the accent hue), with a
**hi-vis safety accent** as the rare ~10%. System-aware **light + dark** (daylight light;
low-light/warehouse dark).
- References: highway/road safety signage, industrial equipment labeling, instrument panels.
- Anti-references: blue corporate SaaS dashboards, generic shadcn neutral-card grids,
  glassmorphism, decorative gradients, maximalist/busy layouts.
- Hard guardrails (from the creator): **no corporate blue**, **not heavy/maximalist**.

### Design Principles
1. **Legible at arm's length** — strong contrast, generous type, no thin gray-on-color.
2. **Forgiving targets** — controls sized for a gloved thumb; status obvious at a glance.
3. **One sharp accent** — hi-vis amber marks *action* and *risk* only; everything else neutral.
4. **Signage, not chrome** — condensed uppercase labels, tabular numerals, thick rules.
5. **Honest states** — empty / saving / flagged / complete each read unambiguously on a job site.

### Tokens (native, `apps/native/src/global.css`)
Auto-switching via `@variant light/dark`. Brand additions for this craft:
`--color-hivis` (safety amber) + `--color-on-hivis`, and semantic `--color-pass` /
`--color-fail`. Neutrals retinted slightly warm (toward hue ~80) for cohesion.
Type: **Barlow Condensed** (headings, section labels, numerals) + **Barlow** (body),
loaded via `@expo-google-fonts`. Signage-grade, utilitarian, not in the AI-default set.
